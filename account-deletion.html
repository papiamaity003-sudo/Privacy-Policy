Data Deletion

Fresh List

Effective date: 22 August 2025

Fresh List does not require user accounts. All shopping list data is stored locally on your device and never uploaded to our servers. However, as part of compliance with Google Play requirements, this page explains how users can delete their data.

How to Delete Shopping List Data

  • Clear the app’s storage from your device settings.
  • Or uninstall the app — this removes all local shopping list data.

How to Delete Advertising Data

Ads in Fresh List are served by Google AdMob. You can manage or delete your advertising data via:

  • Google My Ad Center
  • Reset or delete your Advertising ID in Android device settings.
